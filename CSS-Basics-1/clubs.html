Clubs at PTEC

CyberPatriot

This club is all about hardening operating systems and other cybersecurity practices. After many practices, students compete in the CyberPatriot Competition.

ESports League

Great for competitive gamers, PTEC has ESports teams for Rocket League and League of Legends. Students compete in-game against other students from other schools with ESports teams.

Games Club

Video games such as Mario Kart and Smash Bros Ultimate are played on the big screen in the MPR. Happens once a week after school. Great for casual and competitive gamers alike.

Photography Club

Photography club is good for students who like taking pictures and would like to improve their photography skills. Takes place at lunch, once a week.
