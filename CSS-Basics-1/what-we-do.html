What We Do

(Useful for students to know)

Software Development

Grades 9 and 10

In grade 9, the course is focused on game development with Unity and Visual Studio. In grade 10, the course is focused on web development. Programming languages learned include but are not limited to C#, Python, HTML, CSS and maybe JavaScript.

Networking Essentials

Grade 9

A course taken in grade 9 where you learn about building a simple network, and how the many different types of networks work.

Cybersecurity Essentials

Grade 10

This is an online course, meaning there is no in-class instruction, with the exception of writing quizzes, tests and the final exam. You learn about the many different situations that have happened with the security of computers and networks. You also learn about how to prevent cybersecurity issues from happening in your life, and how to deal with them.

IT Essentials

Grade 10

This course is about building, and fixing many different types of PCs (computers). Upon completing this course, you will be proficient at troubleshooting a wide variety of problems that could arise when working with a computer.
